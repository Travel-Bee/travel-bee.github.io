TravelBee

A travel solution that helps save the planet

Features:

  • A simplistic, colourful UI
  • Points earned based on how eco-friendly your journey is
  • A leaderboard system, showing your points compared to your friends'
  • A global leaderboard showing all users' points
  • Plan a journey, with suggestions on how to make it more eco-friendly
  • See your car's carbon emissions
